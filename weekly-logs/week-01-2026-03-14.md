# Week 1: Build Summary (3/14 – 3/20)

## Project 1: AI Video Pick ([aivideopick.com](http://aivideopick.com))

- First vibe-coded app, distributed this week
- Listed on: Product Hunt, Uneed, Indie Hunt, Tool Directory, etc.
- Results after 7 days: 300+ visitors, 1 signup, 0 payments, 0 usage, free credits untouched
- **Decision:** failed with too low conversion rate, move on

## Project 2: Socrates Finds You (personal consultant tool)

- Second vibe coding project, MVP shipped this week
- **What it does:** an automated pipeline that finds people who need your help. Instead of manually scrolling for clients, it finds them for you.
- **Updates this week:** Added AI-suggested responses for each lead, started using recommended threads to reach out to potential clients
- Fully open source: [github.com/dongzhang84/socrates-finds-you](http://github.com/dongzhang84/socrates-finds-you)

## Project 3: Trend Monitor (view and rank most recent AI tools)

- Existing project, updated twice this week
- Shipped a web UI: [dongzhang84.github.io/trend-monitor](http://dongzhang84.github.io/trend-monitor/)
- **New feature:** Daily analysis of trending products for indie developers — Top 5 get an 8-question deep dive covering target users, monetization, distribution, and whether it's worth building
- Fully open source: [github.com/dongzhang84/trend-monitor](https://github.com/dongzhang84/trend-monitor)

## Key Lessons This Week

- **Set up UTM tracking from day one:** 74% of traffic was "not set", no idea what was working
- **Ship a demo video early** — people landed and left within seconds
- **Distribution is harder than building:** most platforms ban self-promotion or take weeks to review, too slow for a quick go/no-go decision
