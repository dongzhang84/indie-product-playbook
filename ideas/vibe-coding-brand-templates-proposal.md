## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Design Quality Not Good Enough

**Probability:** Medium  
**Impact:** High

**Risk:** Templates look amateurish or still generic.

**Mitigation:**
- Study professional designs extensively
- Iterate based on user feedback
- Consider hiring designer for polish ($500)
- Test with target users before launch
- Start with fewer, higher-quality templates

**Indicator:** Beta testers say "looks generic" or "not professional"

### Risk 2: Vibe Coding Tools Change Format

**Probability:** Medium  
**Impact:** Medium

**Risk:** Lovable/Bolt.new update how they handle design files.

**Mitigation:**
- Use standard formats (W3C DTCG, CSS variables)
- Test with multiple tools (not just one)
- Maintain documentation for manual integration
- Build relationships with tool creators
- Offer updates for format changes

**Indicator:** User reports "templates don't work"

### Risk 3: No Market Demand

**Probability:** Low  
**Impact:** High

**Risk:** Vibe coders don't care about branding.

**Mitigation:**
- Pre-validate with surveys in communities
- Offer free template to gauge interest
- Start with 5 templates (low investment)
- Pivot to custom services if templates don't sell
- Get 10 testimonials before launch

**Indicator:** <5 sales in first week

**If this happens:** Shift to custom template service ($299 each), use templates as portfolio.

### Risk 4: Competition Copies Quickly

**Probability:** High (if successful)  
**Impact:** Medium

**Risk:** Someone launches competing product in Month 3.

**Mitigation:**
- First-mover advantage (build brand)
- Network effects (more templates = more value)
- Quality over quantity (can't compete on speed)
- Build community around product
- Continuous improvement and updates

**Indicator:** Competitor product appears

**Response:** Double down on quality, expand faster, add unique features (customizer, subscription).

### Risk 5: Technical Complexity Too High

**Probability:** Low  
**Impact:** Medium

**Risk:** Agent integration harder than expected.

**Mitigation:**
- Test integration extensively in Week 1
- Simplify if needed (manual copy-paste vs auto-import)
- Clear documentation for any complexity
- Video tutorials showing exact steps
- Offer setup support

**Indicator:** Users can't figure out how to use templates

### Risk 6: Pricing Too High/Low

**Probability:** Medium  
**Impact:** Medium

**Risk:** Price doesn't match perceived value.

**Mitigation:**
- Launch with flexible pricing (A/B test)
- Survey users on willingness to pay
- Offer intro discount (50% off)
- Bundle options for price-sensitive users
- Add premium tier for higher willingness to pay

**Indicator:** High traffic, low conversion OR sales but complaints about price
